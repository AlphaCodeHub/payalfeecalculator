Understanding PayPal Fees

When you receive money through PayPal, there are fees associated with each transaction. The fees vary based on your monthly sales volume and whether the transaction involves international customers.

Monthly Sale

  • Up to $3,000 USD: 4.4% + Fixed Fee
  • $3,000.01 USD - $10,000.00 USD: 3.9% + Fixed Fee
  • $10,000.01 USD - $100,000.00 USD: 3.7% + Fixed Fee
  • Above $100,000.01 USD: 3.4% + Fixed Fee

International Customers (per transaction)

  • 4.4% + Fixed Fee
  • 3.9% + Fixed Fee
  • 3.7% + Fixed Fee
  • 3.4% + Fixed Fee

Please note that actual PayPal fees may vary depending on your account type, transaction currency, and other factors. This calculator provides an estimate based on the specified fee structure.
